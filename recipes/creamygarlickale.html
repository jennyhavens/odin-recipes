Creamy Garlic Kale

Description

This is a recipe that my husband and I love to make as a healthy but delicious side dish. Its creamy and garlicy and simple to make.

It only takes a few simple ingredients and is ready in less than 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 bunch of kale
  • 2 garlic cloves chopped
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup water
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp creme fraiche

Steps

  1. Wash and pat dry the kale. Then pull the leaves off of the stems and dispose of the stems.
  2. Put the kale in a large bowl and tear the kale apart a bit so that there are no large leaves.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large pan on medium-high heat.
  4. When the oil is hot enough add the garlic, salt and pepper and stir constantly for 30 sec - 1 min, or until fragrant.
  5. Add kale and more salt and pepper. Stir occasionally for 2-3 minutes until kale is slightly wilted.
  6. Add butter and water. Stir occasionally for 2-3 minutes until the water is cooked off.
  7. Transfer cooked kale to a bowl and stir in creme fraiche.
  8. Salt and pepper to taste and serve while hot!
